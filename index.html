Questions you might have

And answers we're happy to give

How far in advance should we start planning? +
For weddings and larger celebrations, we recommend reaching out 9 to 12 months ahead. For intimate gatherings, 2 to 3 months is plenty.
Do you work with venues outside your city? +
Absolutely. We've planned events across the country and internationally with our trusted vendor network.
What does your planning fee include? +
Design conception, vendor coordination, timeline management, and on-the-day orchestration. No hidden fees.
We're not sure what we want yet. Is that okay? +
That's where we shine. We'll explore together and uncover what feels right for you.
How do you handle unexpected hiccups? +
With calm and quick thinking. Your job is to be present. Ours is to handle whatever comes up.
