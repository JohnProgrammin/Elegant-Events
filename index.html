Questions you might have

And answers we're happy to give

How far in advance should we start planning? +
For weddings and larger celebrations, we recommend reaching out 9 to 12 months ahead. For intimate gatherings and dinners, 2 to 3 months is often plenty. That said, we've worked magic on shorter timelines too.
Do you work with venues outside your city? +
Absolutely. We've planned events across the country and internationally. Travel is part of what we do, and we have a trusted network of vendors in many regions. Let us know where you're dreaming of.
What does your planning fee include? +
Every package includes design conception, vendor coordination, timeline management, and on the day orchestration. We customize based on what you need. No hidden fees, no surprises. Just clarity from the start.
We're not sure what we want yet. Is that okay? +
That's more than okay. That's where we shine. Many of our clients come to us with a feeling rather than a fully formed vision. We'll explore together and uncover what feels right for you.
How do you handle unexpected hiccups? +
With calm and quick thinking. We always have backup plans and a kit filled with everything from safety pins to stain remover. Your job is to be present. Ours is to handle whatever comes up.
